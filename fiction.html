Fiction Prompt Ideas

Choose one of the prompts below and begin writing in the writing space. These prompts are meant to help you create, explain, and tell fictional stories.

Prompt 1

Every night at midnight, the ancient city of Veylor rearranges itself—streets shift, buildings migrate, entire districts vanish and reappear. Only one person, a young cartographer’s apprentice, can remember the city’s previous layout. When the city begins forming paths that seem to follow them, they realize Veylor is trying to communicate… or warn them of something waking beneath its foundations.

Prompt 2

In a kingdom where every major decision is dictated by the infallible Oracle, a young historian discovers a forbidden truth: the Oracle died centuries ago. The prophecies have been forged by a secret council manipulating the world from the shadows. When the historian confronts them, they’re offered a choice—join the deception and reshape the future, or expose the truth and plunge the kingdom into chaos.

Prompt 3

Once every decade, the moon turns crimson for a single night, unlocking a hidden realm accessible only through mirrors. A crew of thieves—each with a supernatural flaw—plans the greatest heist in history: stealing a relic that can rewrite a person’s past. But when one member secretly intends to erase the event that broke their family, the team must decide whether loyalty or destiny matters more.

Prompt 4

In a small coastal town, storms whisper warnings to a teenage boy—cryptic messages about danger, betrayal, and a coming disaster no one else believes in. When a hurricane forms unnaturally fast and the whispers turn into desperate screams, he realizes the storm isn’t approaching the town… it’s coming for him. And it knows his name.
