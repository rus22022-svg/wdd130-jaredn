Non-Fiction Prompt Ideas

Choose one of the prompts below and begin writing in the writing space. These prompts are meant to help you reflect, explain, and tell real stories from your own life.

Prompt 1

Write about a moment in your life that changed the way you think or see the world.

Prompt 2

Describe a person who has had a major influence on your life and explain why they matter to you.

Prompt 3

Write about a challenge you faced and what that experience taught you.

Prompt 4

Explain a place that is important to you and why it holds meaning in your life.

Writing Page

Pick a non-fiction prompt above, then write your response below.

Writing Space Use this area to draft your response. Focus on real experiences, clear details, and thoughtful reflection.
